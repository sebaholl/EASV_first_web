Best Chocolate Cake Recipe

A rich, moist chocolate cake that everyone will love.

Irresistibly Delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350°F (177°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.

Step 2

In a large bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

Step 3

Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla to the dry ingredients. Mix on medium speed for 2 minutes.

Step 4

Stir in boiling water. The batter will be thin, but that's okay!

Step 5

Pour batter evenly into prepared pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Notes

For extra moisture, cover the cakes with plastic wrap while they cool. This will trap steam and make them even softer.

Tips & Tricks

  • Use high-quality cocoa powder for the best flavor.
  • If you want to add a coffee flavor, substitute half of the water with strong coffee.
  • Frost the cake with chocolate ganache for an extra rich treat.

Nutritional Information
